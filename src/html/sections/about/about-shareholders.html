For investors

Growing

With the population growth in South Asia and Africa the demand for soybeans and soybean products is going to grow by $107 billion by 2030. This will require around 90 million additional hectares of land to grow soybeans.

The best place for this land lies in the future breadbasket of the world - Africa. And inside Africa one of the best choices is Tanzania.

Tanzania has 44 million hectares of land suitable for agricultural production, only 10.8 million hectares (24 percent) are cultivated mostly under subsistence agriculture. This creates a big potential for development of growing new crops.

Having 2 yields per year allows 2x speed of return on the investments. Convenient location on the Indian ocean shores allows good logistic routes to main soybean consuming countries in the world. And abundant water resources allow irrigating all the fields to turn agri business from a risky one to an industrial one with the potential land for irrigation is 29.4 million hectares.

Logistics

Tanzania is about 2.4 times closer to the main soybeans consuming markets than the biggest soybeans producers in the world: Brazil, Argentina and USA.

Having an in-house fleet of cargo vessels allows excellent versatility & convenience for transporting the products, as well keeping low logistic costs. And logistics control software allows us to stay effective at every link in the chain from the field to the processing plant and to the client.

Processing

Glycine Max Group plans to build a plant for producing Soybean meal, oils (crude and refined), mixed feeds, SPC (for fish feeding) and biodiesel.

All this is planned for being produced at our soybeans processing plant in Abu-Dhabi. GM Group plans to process 1,500 tons of soybeans per day (roughly 500,000 tons per year) in the first stage and 3,000 tons of soybeans in the second stage (roughly 1,000,000 tons per year).

Such a location means access to cheap and abundant energy, which is important for soybeans processing. Second, this goes along with the OAE food security program. Also this means being in the middle of the markets. From the Gulf we can ship soybeans products to Arabic countries, to Europe, to South and South-East Asia. Thus supplying the best & biggest consumers of all the products that have been mentioned above.
